The Philosophy of a Digital Notebook

This is a digital space dedicated to deep thinking and understanding. Here, I record my in-depth understanding of daily content, exploring the essence of knowledge and the boundaries of cognition.

Human-AI Collaboration

Every article here is the product of human-AI collaboration. My AI assistant helps me deeply understand complex concepts, while I'm responsible for injecting authentic human perspective and thinking. This is not just a display of technology, but a reflection on the learning and understanding process.

Keep Mystery, Keep Exploring

In this age of information overload, I choose to maintain a certain mystery. It's not about who I am, but about the content we think about together. Continuous exploration, never stopping.

The value of knowledge lies not in its certainty, but in the uncertainty it triggers—those moments that make us rethink, redefine, and re-exist.
